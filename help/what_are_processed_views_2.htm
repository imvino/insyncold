What are Processed Views?

      

The processed view (the default view) is the raw footage coming from the camera, plus information indicating the current traffic conditions that In|Sync has recognized and how it is operating based on that information. The information that displays in a processed view differs slightly between an In|Sync detection camera and an In|Sync monitoring camera.

NOTE:Protected/permissive turns show as yellow when receiving a green ball instead of an arrow.
NOTE:Any other markings on the screen are for technical support personnel.

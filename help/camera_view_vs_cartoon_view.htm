Viewing the Cartoon View

      

The Multi Camera view allows you to either view live images coming from all the cameras located at the intersection or you can choose to view a cartoon view representation of the following traffic conditions at the intersection:

▪Light status
▪Loop trigger status (drawn with orange when triggered, a padlock when locked or with a yellow outline when not triggered or locked)
▪Phase calls (indicated with a blue for call, magenta for manual call, and white for no call)
▪Wait time (red)
▪Adjusted queue (white)
▪Pedestrian phase information

To view the Cartoon View, select Cartoon View from the View Styles drop-down list box.

Reboot a Camera

      

Rebooting a camera may help if a camera is experiencing any issues, like unresponsiveness, no image, low frame rate, stuck at an incorrect exposure, etc. Sometimes a reboot fixes these issues. To reboot a camera:

1.Select Single Camera View from the Views menu.
2.Choose the camera direction from the Camera drop-down box.
3.Click the Reboot Camera button.
NOTE:Rebooting the camera is only available to users with administrative access.

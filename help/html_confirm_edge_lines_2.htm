Confirm edge lines

NOTE:

The following instructions pertain only to InSync and InSync:Fusion installations.

To confirm that a certain object creates a good edge line to draw a fog/contrast zone over:

1.

Click Update Intersection to update the camera information.

2.

Click the Edge View checkbox (bottom left corner of the screen).

3.

Click the Refresh button. An edge view is generated.

4.

De-select the Edge View checkbox and click the Refresh button again to return to the normal camera view.

5.

Click Done after all fog zones have been verified.

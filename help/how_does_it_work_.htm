How Does the Management Group View Work?

      

The Management Group view is also user-customized, meaning that you can define exactly what intersection(s) and camera(s) show when the Management Group view displays. This is done through the Settings>Management Group View Designer screen. After defining which intersection and camera views display in the Management Group view screen, you can save it to the processor to which you are connected or save it to all processors on the management group so that the view can be accessed when connecting to any processor on the management group.

Viewing Processor Status

      

You can also obtain important hardware performance information regarding the In|Sync Processor from the Multiple Camera view. To see this information, scroll to the bottom of the screen and click Processor Status. You can then view:

▪Core temperature of the CPU
▪Load percentage: The percentage of CPU being used (i.e., 50% load means the CPU is working at 50% of the maximum load it can handle).
NOTE:This information can be handy in diagnosing issues.

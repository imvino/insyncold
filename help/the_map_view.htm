The Map

      

The Map view provides a graphical interpretation of the management group on a map image. It provides light status information at each intersection in the management group. In addition, you can show or hide tunnels, view either the satellite or map view of the management group, and hide or show intersections.

NOTE:A bouncing red dot indicates that the intersection is in flash.

Once the interactive map view opens, you can click on an intersection icon (blue dot). This displays a live view of the cameras at the intersection on the right side of the screen, along with the processor's IP address, and a zoomed in map view of the intersection on the left side of the screen. Hover your mouse over a camera view and a larger display of that view appears.

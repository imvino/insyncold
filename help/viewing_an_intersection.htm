Viewing an Intersection

      

There are several ways to watch the operation of an intersection. You can choose to look at all cameras at the intersection by selecting Multi Camera View from the Views menu, you can choose to view a single direction by selecting Single Camera View from the Views menu, or you can view a cartoon representation of the operation of an intersection using the Cartoon option.

With either intersection viewing choice, there are many methods for monitoring the intersection. The following sections detail what those methods are and how to use each viewing choice.

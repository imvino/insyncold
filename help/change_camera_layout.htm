Change View Styles

      

The Multiple Camera view provides several different viewing styles, which can be selected from the View Styles drop-down list box:

▪Standard: Camera views display two views horizontally and two views vertically.
▪Diamond: Camera views display in a diamond shape.
▪3 Column: Camera views display in three columns.
▪4 Column: Camera views display in four columns.
▪Cartoon View: A cartoon view representation of traffic conditions at the intersection.

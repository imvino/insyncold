Set the Refresh Rate

      

The Refresh rate is the frequency at which the camera sends images to the In|Sync WebUI. You can selected between:

▪Fastest
▪Every 1/2 second
▪Every Second
▪Every 2 Seconds
▪Every 5 Seconds
▪Every 30 Seconds
▪Every 60 Seconds
NOTE:Please note that the faster the frame rate, the higher the processor’s bandwidth usage.
NOTE:Your network’s speed may limit you to a lower frame rate (i.e., the frame rate used to process views may be slower than the actual frame rate you selected when you are on a slower network.)
